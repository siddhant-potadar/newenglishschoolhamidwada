Students seeking admission to the 11th standard must meet the following eligibility requirements:

  • Must have passed the SSC (10th) Examination from a recognized board.
  • Students from other boards (CBSE/ICSE) must submit a migration certificate.
  • Admission is subject to merit and availability of seats in the selected stream (Arts or Science).
  • Students must be medically fit and submit a health certificate if requested.
  • Reservation policies will be followed as per government norms.

Note: Admission is provisional until all required documents are verified and approved by the college administration.
