ðŸ“„ Documents Required

Please ensure you have the following documents ready at the time of admission:

  • Original & photocopy of SSC (10th) Marksheet
  • School Leaving Certificate (LC)
  • Migration Certificate (for CBSE/ICSE/Other Board students)
  • Domicile Certificate (if applicable)
  • Category/Caste Certificate (if claiming reservation)
  • Passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Aadhaar Card (photocopy)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship purposes)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate

All documents must be submitted in original along with two sets of photocopies.
